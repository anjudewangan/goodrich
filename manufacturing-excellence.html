Our Modern & Advanced Production Facilities

We believe in offering the best quality products to our customers and strive to achieve this by utilizing cutting-edge technology in every aspect of the manufacturing process.

Goodrich Group has a dedicated team of experts who undertake thorough research to collect information about the finest machinery & equipment available worldwide for the proposed manufacturing activities.

For Goodrich Cereals, the comprehensive research done by the team helped us to set-up an ultra-modern manufacturing campus in
Karnal (Haryana), 2nd campus in Kutail (Haryana) and 3rd campus in Ahmednagar (Maharashtra).

Most of the machinery & equipment employed at the Potato Flake processing plant are imported from the leading manufacturers in Europe. Besides, the Group emphasizes the post-purchase maintenance of the machinery to ensure that product quality never gets compromised in any way.

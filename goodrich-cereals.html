The Cereals Story

A Venture into Excellence

As a strategic addition to the Goodrich Group's diversified ventures, Goodrich Cereals was born out of a visionary move to enter the Food Industry. The establishment of our ultra-modern plant, meeting European standards and equipped with imported machinery in Karnal, marks a significant step towards leadership in the processing of potatoes.

In its inaugural year, Goodrich Cereals made a resounding impact by introducing world-class potato flakes to the Indian market. This move not only positioned us as a key player in the industry but also facilitated a seamless process for farmers to sell their produce and reap the benefits.

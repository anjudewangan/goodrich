The Cereals Story

A Venture into Excellence

As a strategic addition to the Goodrich Group's diversified ventures, Goodrich Cereals was born out of a visionary move to enter the Food Industry. The establishment of our ultra-modern plant, meeting European standards and equipped with imported machinery in Karnal, marks a significant step towards leadership in the processing of potatoes.

In its inaugural year, Goodrich Cereals made a resounding impact by introducing world-class potato flakes to the Indian market. This move not only positioned us as a key player in the industry but also facilitated a seamless process for farmers to sell their produce and reap the benefits.

Our Vision

Elevating Potato Flakes to International Standards

At Goodrich Cereals, our mission is clear – to manufacture potato flakes that adhere to the highest international standards. We take pride in our commitment to excellence at every stage of production. From meticulous procurement of raw materials to rigorous quality checks and impeccable packaging, our dedicated team ensures the delivery of premium potato products.

Diversification of a Conglomerate

Quality Leadership in Potato Flakes

Goodrich Cereals, a star member of the Goodrich Group, aspires to be a leader in the potato flakes business. Our ultra-modern plant, conforming to European standards, and our meticulous processes ensure exceptional quality while upholding the highest hygiene standards. We continue to expand our reach, bringing the goodness of Goodrich Cereals to tables worldwide.

Global Reach

Bringing Quality to Your Table

Goodrich Cereals has emerged as a trailblazer in the export industry, exporting Potato Flakes & other Dehydrated Potato Products in more than 40 Countries. With strategically established offices in Karnal, Delhi, Ahmedabad, London, and Melbourne, our global presence reflects our dedication to meeting the diverse needs of consumers worldwide.

Our clients are partners in our journey, and their success is a testament to the quality and value we bring to the table.
